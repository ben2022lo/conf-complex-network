In this directory,  there are the weekly historical data at the US state-level of several infectious diseases including chlamydia, gonorrhea, influenza, measles, polio, and pertussis. This dataset was previously used in "Scarpino, S.V., Petri, G. On the predictability of infectious disease outbreaks. Nat Commun 10, 898 (2019). https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-019-08616-0" and is freely available at this website (https://github.com/Emergent-Epidemics/infectious_disease_predictability)
